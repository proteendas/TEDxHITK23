About the event

TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. TEDx is a platform used to galvanize and bring a positive and impactful change in a person's belief. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event.

At TEDxHITKolkata, we are known for creating not only a conference but also a Community during the process. Our speakers spark conversations through their creative, innovative, and inspiring messages. Our team of student volunteers makes it their mission to find and disseminate the best ideas to the biggest possible audience.

Speakers

Dr Kunal Sarkar

Eminent Cardiologist

Dr Kunal Sarkar



Dr. Kunal Sarkar is one of the Most Experienced Heart Surgeon in Kolkata. He specializes in Complex Heart Surgeries, ECMO, CABG, and Congenital Heart Disease. He is also the Director and Head of Cardiac Surgery at Medica Superspecialty Hospital, Kolkata. Dr. Sarkar is well known for his high success rate in Coronary, Heart Bypass Surgery with over 18,000 happy patients.

Sujoy Prasad Chatterjee

Actor & Musician

Sujoy Prasad Chatterjee



Sujoy Prasad Chatterjee is an interdisciplinary artist, specializing in spoken words and theatre. He is the founder of SPCkraft, the city's only interdisciplinary arts collective. He voices his expressions on gender, sexuality, social abuse and humanity through his arts. He is the artistic director of PHREEDOM4 EVER MONOLOGUES, India's only solo arts festival. His solo play HAPPY BIRTHDAY had opened in a queer theatre in Toronto and is now a part of an international book on gender and arts.

Nilayan Chatterjee

Music Composer

Nilayan Chatterjee



Nilayan Chatterjee, also known as RJ Neil, is the host of a late night show called ‘Masala Nights’ where he hears confessions made by random callers and as per their requests, delivers their message to a stranger in a musical format. He has also composed music in tollywood films like Dil Khush and Khishmish. The reactions of the people he dedicates his song to make the show all the more riveting and entertaining. Not to forget his endearing opening line: “Yaaro ka yaar, lekar haath mein guitar… that’s Neil for you!”

Anindya Sengupta

Actor & Thespian

Anindya Sengupta



Is currently working as an actor in the Bangla film industry. Anindya has won a Filmfare award for portraying 'Khilat' in the movie 'X=Prem', directed by National Award winning film director, Srijit Mukherjee. Anindya is also a theatre actor and has worked in the hindi film industry too. He has also hosted the Bangla broadcast for the Fifa World Cup 2022 and IPL 2023. He has been working as a television anchor for the last 13 years and has also been a radio jockey for Ishq 104.8 fm.

Shuvro Ghosal

Sports Journalist

Shuvro Ghosal



Shuvro Ghoshal is the founder of The Bridge, India's first digital sports platform dedicated to Olympic Sports. A lifelong sports fan, Shuvro has spent the last 9 years in the digital media industry covering and talking about Indian Sports. With a demonstrated history of executing successful campaigns, he has been recognised by Mark Zuckerberg, and also by Justin Trudeau, Danny Boyle, and international media for a documentary on the Indian Ice Hockey team. A former student of Heritage Institute of Technology, Shuvro has always believed in the proverb ‘Sky is the limit’.

Anubhav Singh

GDE & Founder of DYNOPII
