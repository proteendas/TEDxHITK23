He later on organized a walk and public awareness programme to prevent diabetes on World Diabetes Day 2017, attended by one hundred odd people, across North Calcutta. Dr. Banerjee Regularly organising free diabetes camps for underprivileged patients in the locality and patient awareness camps, focused on diabetes, in various locations around Kolkata.
